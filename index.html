About BuildCraft

Three decades of building Kerala's skyline.

Founded in 1988, BuildCraft has grown from a small contracting firm into Kerala's most trusted construction and real estate company. We've shaped the skylines of Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, Kozhikode, and beyond.

Our team of 800+ professionals — engineers, architects, project managers, and craftsmen — brings unmatched expertise to every project, from a single-family home to a 40-storey tower.

We don't just build structures. We build legacies that families and businesses call home for generations.
